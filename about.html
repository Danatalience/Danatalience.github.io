Welcome!

Greetings! My name is Natalie, and I am a semi-professional photographer based in the Midwest, dedicated to capturing your cherished memories. I am two months away from receiving my Bachelor's degrees in Communication and Dance at Hope College (Holland, MI) with a career in wedding/event planning and photography waiting for me! Having grown up in a creative family where dance and music played a significant role, I discovered my passion for photography during a challenging period in my dance career when I sustained an injury. I took on the role of team photographer, which ignited my enthusiasm for this art form. I specialize in various photography styles, including events, couples, headshots, graduations, nature, and sports. I invite you to explore my portfolio to gain insight into my artistic vision, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
